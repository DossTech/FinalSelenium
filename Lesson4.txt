TestNG:
-------
1.Featured to execute testcases sequentially
2.Featured to execute testcases parallely
3.Gives the status at the end of each status
4.Given execution summary report(HTML Report)
5.Support Data Driven - to execute with multiple daa
6.Support cross browser testing
7.Can control the execution flow with certain annotations and attributes
8.Provides Listeners (Retry failed testcases)
9.Assertions to handle validations and verifications


Dependencies:
--------------
1.Selenium-java
2.TestNG : 7.4.0
